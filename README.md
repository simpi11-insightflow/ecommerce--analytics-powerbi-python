🔍Project Overview
This project analyzes an e-commerce dataset to uncover key business insights related to revenue ,customer behaviour, and delivery performance.Data was cleaned,transformed and engineered using Python and insights were visualized through an interactive Power BI dashboard.

🎯Business Objective 
-Identify revenue drivers and growth patterns.
-Analyze delivery performance and customer experience.
-Understand customer and payment behaviour.
-Detect operational inefficiencies impacting business performance.

🔨Tools and Technologies
-Python(Pandas,Data Cleaning,Feature Engineering)
-Power BI(Dashboard & Visualization)
-Excel(Data Storage)
-GitHub(Project Documentation)

📁Dataset
-Multiple dataset:orders,customers,payment,order items.

📊Dashboard Preview
-📈Key Insights
-Revenue peaks mid-year and declines sharply in Q4, indicating strong seasonality and demand fluctuation
-Revenue is highly concentrated in a few cities, showing dependency on limited geographic regions
-Credit card dominates payment methods, creating risk due to lack of diversification
-Average delivery time (~12 days) suggests operational inefficiency compared to industry standards
-Late deliveries exist despite high on-time %, indicating inconsistency in logistics performance

🚨Business Problem,impacts and recommendation 
1.Delivery Delay
Problem: Orders are delayed beyond estimated delivery dates
Impact: Poor customer experience, reduced repeat purchases
Recommendation:
-Optimize logistics routes
-Improve last-mile delivery efficiency
-Set realistic delivery expectations

2. Revenue Concentration Risk
Problem: Revenue is heavily dependent on a few cities
Impact: High business risk if demand drops in key regions
Recommendation:
-Expand into new cities
-Run targeted marketing campaigns in low-performing regions

3. Payment Dependency
Problem: Majority of transactions via credit card
Impact: Risk of payment failure and limited customer flexibility
Recommendation:
-Promote alternative payment options 
-Offer discounts for diversified payment usage

4. Seasonal Revenue Drop
Problem: Significant decline in revenue during Q4
Impact: Unstable revenue growth
Recommendation:
-Launch seasonal campaigns and offers
-Improve customer retention strategies

This analysis helps stakeholders:
